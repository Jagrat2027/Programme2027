## 📌 Cross-cutting Assumptions

- 35 million households affected by the tax overhaul  
- Around 2 million citizens impacted by the merged income-tax/contribution line  
- Over 100 tax loopholes removed by 2030  
- Public spending > €1M fully traceable via citizen portals  
- 100 local citizen funds created for reinvestment of tax revenues  
- Tiered VAT applied to all major retailers by 2029  
- Open-source fiscal education tools deployed in schools

---

## 📈 Targets by 2030

- Significant reduction in tax inequality (max effective rate gap x5)  
- Genuine progressivity restored on all income types  
- End of systemic tax avoidance for firms with >250 employees  
- Stronger public acceptance via clarity and traceability  
- Fiscal rewards for virtuous and solidarity-based behavior  
- Constitutional recognition of commons as legitimate tax lever
